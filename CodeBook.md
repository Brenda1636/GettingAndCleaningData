Data Dictionary for Tidy Data Set created in Course Project 
for Getting and Cleaning Data from the Samsung Galaxy Smart Phone Data
Brenda Chodkowski
======================

<b>subject</b>
     The Subject who participated in the study. There were 30 volunteers between the ages of 19 - 48 years.
          1...30 

<b>activity</b>
     The activity the partipant completed while wearing a smart phone.
          1 WALKING
          2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
          3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
          4 SITTING
          5 STANDING
          6 LAYING

<b>tBodyAcc-mean()-X</b> 
     The mean of the means of subject's acceleration signal for the body on the X-axis.

<b>tBodyAcc-mean()-Y</b>
     The mean of the means of subject's acceleration signal for the body on the Y-axis.

<b>tBodyAcc-mean()-Z</b>
