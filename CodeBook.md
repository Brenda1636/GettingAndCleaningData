<b>subject</b><br>
     The Subject who participated in the study. There were 30 volunteers between the ages of 19 - 48 years.
          1...30<br> 
<br>
<b>activity</b><br>
     The activity the partipant completed while wearing a smart phone.<br>
          1 WALKING<br>
          2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS<br>

          4 SITTING<br>
          5 STANDING<br>
